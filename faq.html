Frequently Asked Questions


Is it compulsory for everyone to enroll for a COVID-19 vaccination passport?

Nope it is not mandatory.

Any affiliation to any medical hospitals or government organizations?

Not at this point.

How will data collected be utilised?

Non-essential information will be masked out and data collected will be analyzed from authorised personnel of the following sectors:

1) Government

2) Immigration and border control

3) Medical professionals

How can we ensure data integrity on the vaccination certificate captured?

We have staff deployed at across all vaccination sites. Right after you have received your second dose of vaccine, our staff would then assist on site with the certificate upload process.
For more detailed information, kindly refer to the Home Page with regards to the above as described under the Steps to Onboard section.

How can I access my COVIDIENT-19 passport during my travel?

You may retrieve your COVID-19 vaccination eletronic certificate on thie site and it should be also made available on your SingPass app.

Is COVIDIENT-19 liable should I develop medical allergies from getting vaccinated?

Should you develop any allergies or uncover any symptoms, please approach your nearest GP or the vaccination location immediately.
Please be advised to check out the MOH website at www.moh.gov.sg/covid-19/vaccination should you have any urgent queries.
